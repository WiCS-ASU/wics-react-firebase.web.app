Recurring Events

What WiCS hosts.

Community

General Body Meetings

Regular member gatherings with club updates, activities, networking, and ways to get involved in WiCS.

Industry

Tech Talks

Sessions with engineers, researchers, and industry guests sharing technical topics and career insights.

Connection

Socials

Low-pressure events where members can relax, meet friends, and build community beyond classes.

Career Prep

Technical Interview Prep Sessions (TIPS)

Practice-focused sessions for coding interviews, problem solving, resumes, and recruiting readiness.

Competition

WiCS Annual Programming Competition

An annual programming competition with industry that builds a community of hands-on problem solvers tackling technical challenges.

Outreach

K-12 Outreach

Programs that introduce younger students to computing through accessible, encouraging activities.

Visibility

Tablings

Campus outreach moments where students can meet WiCS, ask questions, and learn about upcoming opportunities.

Celebration

End-of-Semester Banquet

A closing celebration to recognize the semester, thank contributors, and spend time together as a community.

Support

Desert CodeSprouts

A hands-on computing workshop for middle-school students exploring programming, cybersecurity, machine learning, and more, supported by WiCS volunteers.
