Farideh Tadayon-Navabi

Farideh Tadayon-Navabi is a principal lecturer in the School of Computing, Informatics, and Decision Systems Engineering. Prior to ASU, she held a teaching position at University of Louisiana in Monroe.

Her work focuses on improving undergraduate curriculum, helping students succeed in introductory courses, and retaining students in computing programs.

Samira Ghayekhloo

Samira Ghayekhloo is an assistant teaching professor at the School of Computing and Augmented Intelligence at Arizona State University. She earned her PhD in Artificial Intelligence and has over ten years of teaching experience.

Before joining ASU, she served as an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Engineering at Azad University, Iran, mentoring graduate and undergraduate students.
